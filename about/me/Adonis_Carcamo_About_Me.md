# About Me — Adonis Carcamo

*Public narrative synthesized from Cosa Bora Command Center profile / founder bank (2026-07-14). No phone numbers or private grant tactics. Softened CC overclaims for investor honesty.*

---

**Adonis Carcamo** is the Founder, Creator, and Visioneer of **Rozi-X** — and the operator who refused to let an assistant call something “done” when the real world disagreed.

He is a first-generation Salvadoran-American, raised in Texas by immigrant parents who became naturalized U.S. citizens. That background shows up in how he builds: start from nothing, keep the standard high, and do not accept “close enough” when the stakes are real.

He is deliberately **not** a programmer. Since COVID he has built businesses to survive and grow — including **Pombomb Media**, a live-streaming and event-production house that works with B2B clients, community events, and municipal media needs. Under the **Cosa Bora** umbrella he is standing up **Platysonique** as the AI/software home for Rozi, with additional creative production planned separately.

Rozi began as the assistant he needed to run that life: trading, research, calendar, mail — work that has to happen while he is busy making other things. When the system started reporting success that had not actually happened, he did not shrug. He made verification the law of the product: nothing is accepted without live proof. For roughly a year he directed AI coding and research systems to build under that standard, catching every flattering “98% done” that still failed against reality.

He lives and works in the Highland Village / Lewisville, Texas area. He speaks English and Spanish. His role on Rozi is founder, product architect, and truth-enforcer — the human framework that decides what counts as finished.
